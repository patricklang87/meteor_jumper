On a computer, move the spaceman with the arrow keys. Use the B key to hold still while the asteroids advance.

On a smartphone, tap above or below, or to the right or to the left of the spaceman to move him. Tap on him to keep him still while the asteroids advance.

You can squeeze between diagonally adjacent asteroids. Flying into an adjecent oncoming asteroid allows you to jump it! Catch a lightning bolt to gain a life!
